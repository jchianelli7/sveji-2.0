About
Sveji Boyz is a hip-hop duo comprised of two brothers from Milwaukee, WI. NK Sveji, born June 18, 2003 is the main vocalist, while Sveji Tosh, born on July 10th, 1997 is the lead engineer for the group. Sveji Boyz come from a Russian heritage, and the name “Sveji” (Свежий) actually means ‘Fresh.’ Together, the two brothers set out to make a name for themselves. Prior to forming Sveji Boyz, Sveji Tosh (alias Tony Freeloader) released a solo rap project named Stone Table Summer in 2014. In the fall of 2017, Tosh studied at the School of Audio Engineering in Chicago in order to hone his skills in a studio environment. Currently, Tosh works at the Chicago Recording Company training to be an assistant engineer. With a wide range of talents, from writing to producing to engineering, Tosh is key to all things behind the scenes of the Sveji sound. NK Sveji, frontman for Sveji Boyz, is an artist, dancer, and model. He is also a saxophone player who studied jazz with Milwaukee’s Youth Symphony Orchestra. Before re-branding as NK Sveji, he used the name NkDances to upload dancing videos to social media looking for recognition. In the past years, NK has also taken up modeling to set himself apart and make an extra money on the side. In the future, we hope to incorporate all of his talents on a larger platform. Sveji Boyz released their first body of work, entitled “Take 5ive” in January 2019 and are working on a second project scheduled for release in May.
Service
Sveji Boyz would like to present Sveji Services. This service will provide aspiring artists the chance to work at the Legendary Chicago Recording Company with Sveji Tosh. There is an hourly fee for studio time which can be sent electronically. To start out, we are proposing a three track session. Feel free to e-mail us with any other questions or to book your time now!
